Lola Amour

Lola Amour is a Filipino rock band from Muntinlupa, Philippines that dabbles in the genres of modern rock, funk, and pop. The band consists of Pio Dumayas on lead vocals and guitar, Martin Kim on background vocals and keyboards, Raymond King on background vocals and bass, Zoe Gonzales on lead guitar, Angelo Mesina on trumpets, Joxx Perez on saxophone, and Renzo Santos on drums.[1] They also introduced three sessionists into the band as regular performers: Jeff Abueg on saxophone, Raffy Perez on drums, and Manu Dumayas on trumpet.
